The Best Movies According to Josh McCleary

Here are my favorite movies of all time:

Forrest Gump

This movie came out the summer between my high school and college years. Tom Hanks gave one of the most memorable performances ever and the plot was unique and amazing.

Rocky (1-4)

One of the best underdog stories of all time. They are all great until the 5th one!

The Shawshank Redemption

The writing and acting in this one put it over the top, along with the plot twist at the end.

Ferris Bueller's Day Off

I had to add a funny one to the list. This classic comedy has one-liners from beginning to end.
